About Us

Connectize solves sector fragmentation, creating a secure, centralized corporate media platform for the Nigerian and African oil & gas industry.

Our Mission

To empower the oil, gas, and energy sectors across Nigeria and Africa on a global scale by creating a trusted digital ecosystem that connects verified companies, fosters collaboration, enhances visibility, and drives sustainable business growth.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s and Africa’s leading corporate networking and business facilitation platform for the oil and gas industry by driving innovation, transparency, and human capital development through trusted digital connections.

Our Strategy

Unifying Ecosystem: Unifying isolated players, expanding Africa's energy workforce, and driving human capital development through skilled services export

FAQs

What industry does connectize focus on

Initially Oil and Gas in Nigeria/Africa. Planning to expand to manufacturing, Agriculture and ICT.

Who is the Target clientele for connectize?

Our target clientele includes major oil and gas operators, local and international service providers, regulatory bodies (NUPRC, NCDMB), and investors in the African energy sector.

Is Connectize Endorse by any regulatory?

Connectize is built with full compliance in mind and works closely with regulatory bodies like the NUPRC (Nigerian Upstream Petroleum Regulatory Commission) to ensure our platform supports mandated industry requirements and standards.

How does connectize generates revenue?

Revenue is generated primarily through subscription tiers for premium business services, verification and compliance checks, and secure transaction fees processed within the platform.

How does Connectize Helps me find Credible partners?

We implement a rigorous, multi-factor verification process for all corporate users, checking regulatory compliance status, certifications, and industry track records, ensuring you only connect with vetted and credible partners.
